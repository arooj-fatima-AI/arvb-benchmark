# AI Retrievability & Visibility Benchmark (ARVB) v1.1

## Overview

The **AI Retrievability & Visibility Benchmark (ARVB)** is a standardized evaluation framework used to assess whether and how an entity is structurally discoverable, interpretable, and reusable by AI answer engines, knowledge systems, and retrieval environments.

ARVB does not measure marketing performance, traffic, rankings, or commercial outcomes.

It measures:

- Entity clarity
- Retrieval readiness
- Structural consistency
- Authority reinforcement capacity
- Answerability integrity

ARVB exists within the discipline of:

# AI Retrieval & Visibility Architecture

This category focuses on designing structured, verifiable, machine-readable authority systems that allow organizations and defensible experts to be accurately retrieved, interpreted, cited, and trusted by AI systems such as:

- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Claude
- Perplexity
- Grok
- Google AI Overviews
- Knowledge Graph systems

ARVB is the measurement layer of this discipline.

## What ARVB Measures

ARVB evaluates structural authority readiness across **8 fixed dimensions**:

1. Entity Clarity  
2. Data Layer Presence  
3. Chunkability  
4. Semantic Clarity  
5. Authority Signals  
6. Consistency  
7. Answerability  
8. Risk & Gaps

These dimensions collectively determine AI retrieval behavior.

The benchmark is:

- Outside-in
- Structurally deterministic
- Based on public information only
- Focused on AI-mediated discoverability

It is not:

- SEO scoring
- Content marketing evaluation
- AI training evaluation
- Growth experimentation

---

## Scoring System

ARVB uses:

# Binary Checkpoints → Forced Score Mapping

Interpretation is not allowed.

Each dimension is evaluated using:

- Fixed conditions
- Binary validation (YES / NO)
- Locked score outputs

This ensures consistency across audits and removes subjective interpretation.

Final scoring range:

| Score | Classification |
|---|---|
| 0–10 | Invisible |
| 11–20 | Weakly Discoverable |
| 21–30 | Moderately Retrievable |
| 31–40 | Strong AI Presence |

These classifications describe retrieval readiness only.

- Longitudinal structural authority tracking
- ARVO implementation diagnostics
